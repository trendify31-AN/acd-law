United Arab Emirates

The UAE is a constitutional federation of seven emirates that was established on December 10th, 1971. At the crossroad between West and East, the UAE is a politically and economically stable country and holds the 25th position globally in the Global Competitiveness Report 2019 by the World Economic Forum, at the heart of exchanges and business relations. Recognized as a tolerant country, the UAE hosts more than 200 nationalities doing business, living, learning, and touring the UAE based on the vision of its First President, the late H. H. Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan, who created a unique model of society and coexistence. At ACD Law, we embrace the same vision and values, and therefore, our team is eager to assist and advise our clients based on those principles.
